Sundried Tomato Pasta Salad

Description:

A simple vegetarian cold pasta salad recipe.

Use any type of pasta. Medium shells are typically the best (even though the photo show bow pasta).

Time: About 20 minutes or so.

Ingredients:

There's no specific measurements here, just do your thing, girl.

  • Pasta (whatever type)
  • Spinach
  • Olive Oil
  • Pesto
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Sea Salt

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package's intructions.
  2. Drain pasta and run cold water over it to cool it.
  3. Transfer pasta to a serving bowl or mixing bowl if doing several servings.
  4. Throw in the spinach (like actaully throw it in).
  5. Pour a sensible amount of olive oil and mix it together.
  6. Add the pesto.
  7. Throw in the sundried tomatoes and feta cheese and mix it up, baby.
  8. Garnish with parmesan and sea salt.
  9. Proceed to mix in the garnish and just eat it.
