Rice Pudding

Description:

Eat it as a snack, breakfast, hell even dinner or with a glass of craft beer. You do you.

The following recipe will get you about 2-3 servings.

Time: About 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp of rice (long, medium, or short grain)
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 1/8 tsp or pinch of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp of vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Add rice and water to a nonstick pan.
  2. Add salt and stir.
  3. Cook on medium high heat until it boils.
  4. Once it boils, turn down the heat to medium low.
  5. Cover pan with a lid and let it simmer while stirring now and then until rice is soft.
  6. Once all the water has been absorbed, stir in the milk and sugar and turn up heat to medium.
  7. Keep stirring until it has thickened. Remember, it will thicken even more as it cools.
  8. When it starts bubbling a lot, keep stirring to avoid burning on the bottom.
  9. When it has thickened to like a thick porridge or watery oat meal, turn off the heat and continue to stir.
  10. Once the bubbles subsde, add in the vanilla extract and stir.
  11. Let it cool and then serve or pack away.
